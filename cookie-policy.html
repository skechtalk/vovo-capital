What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us understand how visitors interact with our site, remember your preferences, and improve your overall experience.

Types of Cookies

Managing Cookies

You can accept or reject non-essential cookies at any time by clearing your browser's cookies and reloading the site, which will trigger the consent banner again.

You can also manage or delete cookies directly through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect certain features of the website.
